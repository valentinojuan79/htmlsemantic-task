Profile

Charles Leclerc , born on October 16, 1997, in Monte Carlo, Monaco. He began karting at a young age and quickly progressed through junior categories, winning the GP3 Series in 2016 and the FIA Formula 2 Championship in 2017. Leclerc made his F1 debut in 2018 with Sauber before joining Ferrari in 2019, where he quickly became a leading driver for the team. His teammate is currently Lewis Hamilton. Known for his speed, precision, and fearless overtaking, Leclerc is regarded as one of the brightest young talents in Formula One.

Achievements

  • 1x Formula Two Champion
  • 27 pole positions
  • 8 Grand Prix wins
  • 48 podium finishes
