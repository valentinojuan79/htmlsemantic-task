Profile

Michael Schumacher , born January 3, 1969, in Germany, is a retired Formula One driver and seven-time World Champion. Debuting in 1991, he won two titles with Benetton and five consecutive championships with Ferrari from 2000–2004. Renowned for his speed, skill, and competitiveness, Schumacher is considered one of F1’s all-time greats.

Achievements

  • 7x Formula One World Champion, joint record with Lewis Hamilton
  • 68 pole positions.
  • 91 Grand Prix wins.
  • 155 podium finishes.
