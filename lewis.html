Profile

Lewis Hamilton , born January 7, 1985, in England, is a British Formula One driver and seven-time World Champion. Debuting in 2007 with McLaren, he later moved to Mercedes, dominating the sport with multiple titles. He is currently racing for Scuderia Ferrari. His teammate is currently Charles Leclerc. Renowned for his speed, consistency, and advocacy for diversity and the environment, Hamilton is one of Formula One most iconic figures.

Achievements

  • 7x Formula One World Champion, joint-record with Michael Schumacher
  • Holds the record for most pole positions in F1
  • 105 Grand Prix wins
  • 202 podium finishes
