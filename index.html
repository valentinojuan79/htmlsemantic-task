History

Scuderia Ferrari, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, is based in Maranello, Italy. It is the racing division of the Ferrari in Formula One. It is the oldest and most successful team in Formula One history, having competed since the inaugural season in 1950. With it's iconic red cars, Ferrari stands a symbol of speed, passion, and Italian pride.

Achievements

  • 16 Constructors' Championships
  • 15 Drivers' Championships
  • 238 Grand Prix wins
  • Over 1,000 podium finishes
